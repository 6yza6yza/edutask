At Eduarkiva, we take the privacy of our users' personal data very seriously. This privacy statement explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal data of our users.

Purpose of the Repository

Eduarkiva is a digital repository established by Edutask Institute that aims to collect, store and share Educational research data, resources, and scholarly outputs. Our repository is meant to facilitate collaboration among researchers and provide access to high-quality resources for educational purposes and research.

Types of Data Collected

We may collect personal data such as names, email addresses, and other identifying information from our users. We may also collect non-personal data such as IP addresses and user activity data for the purpose of improving our services.

Data Collection Methods

We collect personal data directly from our users through registration forms, surveys, and other interactions. We may also collect non-personal data automatically through the use of cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies.

Use of Data

We use personal data to provide and improve our services, such as to enable collaboration among researchers and to provide access to educational resources. We may also use personal data to communicate with our users and to inform them of new services or updates to our repository. We may share personal data with third-party service providers that help us operate our repository, but we will never sell personal data to third parties.

Security Measures

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use secure servers, encryption, and access controls managed by Kosovo Research and Education Network to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of personal data.

User Rights

Users have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal data. Users can also object to the processing of their personal data or request a restriction of processing. Users can exercise these rights by contacting research@edu-task.com. We will respond to these requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices or this privacy statement, please contact us at research@edu-task.com.

We reserve the right to update or modify this privacy statement at any time without prior notice. The most recent version of the privacy statement is available on our website.
