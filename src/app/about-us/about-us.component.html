About Kosovo Digital Educational Archive (KDEA)

The Kosovo Digital Educational Archive contains documents published by governments, and other relevant bodies, authorities and education institutions in Kosovo in different historical periods. The archive collects documents in print and in electronic format from different periods in order to create, preserve and make accessible for the public and research community, important documents in the field of education. Our aim is to create a searchable digital archive by periods, year, institutions, communities of practice and authors free of charge and accessible to all. The preservation of documentary heritage in the field of education was and is still affected by changes in the administration of education throughout history, consequences of war, archiving practices and broken website links. Many important documents are not easily accessible for the general public and researchers, jeopardizing the collective memory preservation.
