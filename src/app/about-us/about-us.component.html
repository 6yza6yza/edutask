About Us

Edutask Institute is a non-profit entity engaging in quality research and innovative education solutions.

Eduarkiva as a documentary heritage initiative links disciplines such as education, culture, humanities, and information sciences to collect, preserve and make accessible important documents for communities.

We believe that educational documentary heritage can contribute to more conscious understanding and promotion of history as well as to peace building and constructive remembrance. The space created within Eduarkiva offers tools and opportunities for the development of scientific debate, creativity, social cohesion and cultural dialogue.

The first phase of this project is supported by the Municipality of Prishtina through a grant to establish the archive and initiate the collection covering the period of 1990’s.

Hosting and data storage is enabled by the Kosovo National Research and Education Network.

About Kosovo Digital Educational Archive (KDEA) Collection

The repository includes documents published by the Kosovo Government, Kosovo Parliament, Ministry of Education and other institutions and departments working in the area of Education.

All documents are added according to the Law on Access to Public Documents, which allows us to put information of the public domain and to convert them to PDF formats to enable permanent preservation as long as we attribute the resource.

In trying to digitise physical archive collections in municipal and national archive, we are committed to cooperate with institutions to support the digitisation process according to the permission rights, to share the educational related documents. Edutask Institute establishes cooperation agreements with institutions to reproduce and share different collections.

The repository includes:

  • - Laws
  • - Administrative instructions
  • - Decisions
  • - Policy Documents
  • - Annual reports
  • - Research reports
  • - Speeches
  • - Statistics
  • - Datasets
  • - Images
  • - Videos

How can you cooperate with us?

If you would like to find out more about Eduarkiva, please contact us at: research@edu- task.com

There are several possibilities for you to contribute to the project.

If you are an author/researcher in the field of education, please register and upload your contributions. In the author section your orchid account can be automatically linked to the archive.

If you are a collector, or have important documents relevant for documentary education heritage, please register and upload the document as contributors. Your document will be preserved in the digital archive, whereas the original document should be sent to the Municipal or National Archive.

If you are an institution (Archive, Research Institute, NGO) please register as a community and upload your documents and publications. If you would like to cooperate with us to digitise any collection in the field of education, please contact us at: research@edu-task.com
